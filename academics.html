Academics at Greenfield School

We provide a comprehensive and balanced curriculum that equips our students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in life. Our academic programs encourage creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Curriculum Overview

Level Subjects Offered
Primary (Grades 1–5) English, Math, Science, Arts, Physical Education
Middle (Grades 6–9) English, Math, Science, Social Studies, IT, Arts, PE
Secondary (Grades 10–12) Advanced Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Business, Literature, ICT

Key Academic Areas

Science

Hands-on experiments and lab work encourage discovery and innovation.

Technology

From coding to robotics, we prepare students for the digital future.

Arts

Music, drama, and fine arts nurture creativity and expression.

Sports

A wide range of sports ensures fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills.
